Our Services

We offer a variety of services aimed at simplifying the management of PFE, making the process easier for our users.

Adding Projects

Both entreprises and teachers can add projects for the students to choose for their PFE and these subjects get accepted through the head of department.These subjects are the start of the PFE.

Choosing Projects

Students can find available subjects to work on on this platform without having to look for projects on each entreprise's website and without having to go to teachers to get subjects

24h/7 Support and supervision

Teachers can supervise and give their advices to students through the platform, Entreprises can supervice the projects and students get advices.

We Are PFE-HUB

Our platform serves as a central hub for final-year project allocation, simplifying the process for both students and academic institutions. Through a user-friendly interface, it facilitates seamless coordination, enabling students to explore project proposals and express their preferences efficiently. With features like project categorization and real-time updates, transparency and fairness are upheld in project assignment. Our commitment to optimizing the allocation process empowers students to pursue projects aligned with their interests and academic goals, fostering an environment conducive to impactful research and innovation.
